SAME CREW KC is a collective of artists, musicians, and creatives based in Kansas City, Missouri. We are dedicated to creating a space for artists to collaborate, share resources, and support each other in their creative endeavors. Our mission is to build a community of artists who are passionate about their work and committed to making a positive impact on the world around them. We believe that art has the power to inspire, educate, and transform lives, and we are committed to using our talents to make a difference in our community and beyond.
